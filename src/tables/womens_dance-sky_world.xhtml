Yoho:h gëöya’ge:h yohtë:joh (vocable) in the Sky World that’s what they do
niyögwayo’dëh jögwe’o:weh the way that we are doing it the Real People
awë:notgäh nitši:yo:h niyögwano:h negaewënyeh it’s good fun and it occupies our minds when we’re doing it
