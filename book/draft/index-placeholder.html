Songs at the Woods’ Edge

The Earth Songs of the Seneca Nation

Nya:wëh sgë:nö’. This site creates a space for sharing traditional music and dance of the Onöndowa’ga:’ (Seneca) Nation, one of the Six Nations of the Haudenosaunee (Iroquois) Confederacy. The site will feature video and audio recordings of Seneca Earth Songs (Yöëdza’ge:ka:’ gaë:nö’shö’) together with traditional teaching and historical information.

This is a collaborative project between Seneca faithkeeper Bill Crouse, Sr., and musicologist Andrew Cashner of the University of Rochester.
